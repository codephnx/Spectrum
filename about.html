Our approach to beauty is simple. We know each client comes to us with a unique spectrum of expectations and ideas about beauty. We aim to reflect and magnify that spectrum with each interaction – knowing that seasons, styles and ideas are always changing. Spectrum offers a wide range of individualized services including haircuts, color, texturizing, shampoos, conditioning treatments, blow dry and styling, hair and lash extensions, skin care and waxing. The salon’s Wash House and private rooms provide ultimate comfort during shampoo, conditioning, skin care and waxing services.
